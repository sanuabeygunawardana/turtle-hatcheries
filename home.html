INTRODUCTION

Turtles have important roles in freshwater and wetland ecosystems as herbivores, predators, and scavengers in food webs. Srilanka has various species of turtles habitating in coastal areas. Wildlife experts has taken the responsibilty of protecting these wonderful creatures by establishing turtle hatcheries around the country.

How to spend quality time while visiting the Hikkaduwa turtle hatchery...?

  • Go on a private tour around the fabulous Galle city with your friends or family.
  • Pay a visit to the cinnamon and rubber plantation by tuktuk.


Volunteering in a sea turtle hatchery

Most of the turtle hatcheries in Srilanka welcomes volunteery helpers to spend a day with these lovable creatures and gives everyone an opprotunity to take care of the sea turtles. If you are a animal loving person, then this activity is specially recommended to you, your family and friends to have a fun time and learn more about our turtles.
