KOSGODA TURTLE HATCHERY

Another significant attraction is the Turtle Hatchery at Bentota Beach. This untouched beach has long been a favorite of up to five endangered turtle species. Before being released into the wild, the turtles are given the opportunity to hatch and grow in a safe environment. For those who enjoy the outdoors, a trip to the turtle hatchery is a need.
